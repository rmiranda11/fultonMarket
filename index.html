Prologue

Located at 205 N. Peoria, Fulton Market Co. is a new restaurant and bar in the well-know West Loop restaurant mecca, but the inspiration started here many decades ago. Our father proudly brought home the finest selection of pork chops, steaks, and chicken from the butcher shop where he worked, inspiring new takes on family classics. Today, we’d like to share a taste of our family’s nightly dining experience with you by providing meze style plates, an upbeat yet casual restaurant atmosphere, craft beers, and unique signature cocktails. Come as you are, you’re welcome here at Fulton Market Company.

Private Events

Fulton Market Co offers dining for any size group up to 150 people, whether it be a small get together, a birthday celebration, or a full wedding reception our space offers the perfect chance for a marvelous experience. Our space is popular for being in the heart of The Fulton Market District, surrounded by great restuarants and entertainment. The building has large open garage style windows that makes it extremly enjoyable during warm seasons.

For more information and to plan your next special event please fill out the contact form below. Our Special Events team will contact you to confirm availablity.
